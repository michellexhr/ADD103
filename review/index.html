Zodiac Academy: The Awakening

I will be reviewing my most recent read, which is Zodiac Academy. This series consists of 7 books as of now with the 8th book coming soon, but today I will only be reviewing the first book in the series. This series is focused on the Vega twins while also giving us other's points of view.

The Vega Twins

Darcy and Tory Vega are the main female characters of this series and they're twins. Their life was pretty crazy growing up and when they turned 18 their life took an unexpected turn for the worst/best. They were the rightful heirs to the throne in Solaria and even though they only wanted a better life for themselves, they now needed to learn how to wield their powers as well as endure the living hell the celestial heirs were going to unleash upon them.

Principal Nova

The twins get told by Principal Nova that their birth names are Roxania and Gwendalina and Tory was not going to accept going by any name other than Tory.

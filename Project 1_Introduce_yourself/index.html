About me

Hi, my name is Michelle! This is my first year attending McHenry County College. I am pursuing my A.A.S in Graphic Design. These are some of my interest/hobbies and just a peek into my life.

I'm a mom to a 2 year old who is very social and makes my introverted self more extroverted. I started attending college because I wanted to gain knowledge and pursue a career that I would enjoy working in, as well as being able to give my son a better life. When I'm able to have some time to myself I often find myself reading or making art. Some of my favorite genres are:

  • Psychological Thriller
  • Romance
  • Utopian/Dystopian

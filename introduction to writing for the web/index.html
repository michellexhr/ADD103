Lash Out! A Fun Guide to Eyelash Extensions

What Are Eyelash Extensions?

Eyelash extensions are tiny fake lashes that are glued onto your natural lashes. A lash expert applies them one by one to give your lashes a longer, fuller look — no mascara needed!

You can choose a simple, everyday look or go for something more dramatic or elegant for special events. Here are your style options:

  • Simple or dramatic styles
  • Different lengths and thicknesses
  • Materials like silk, mink, or synthetic

How Long Do They Last?

Eyelash extensions last about 2 to 4 weeks. They fall out naturally as your real lashes shed. To keep the look fresh and flawless, many people get a “fill” every 2–3 weeks.

Are They Safe?

Yes — when done by a trained and licensed professional. But there are some risks to be aware of:

  • Allergic reactions to the glue
  • Damage to natural lashes if the extensions are too heavy
  • Infections if tools aren’t clean

How to Take Care of Your Lashes

To keep your extensions looking fabulous — whether you chose a simple or elegant style — follow these tips:

  • Don’t rub your eyes
  • Avoid oil-based makeup and removers
  • Keep them dry for the first 24 hours
  • Gently brush them daily with a clean spoolie

Should You Try Them?

Eyelash extensions are a fun way to feel extra glam. Whether you're going for a simple everyday look, a dramatic evening style, or something soft and elegant for a big event — there's a lash style for everyone!

If you're curious, visit a trusted lash technician and ask questions before you commit.

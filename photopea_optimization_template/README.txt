Web Image Optimization — Student Template
========================================

HOW TO USE
1) Put your OWN photos into the images/ folder.
2) In index.html, replace the placeholder image paths and alt text.
3) For each technique section (Crop, Resize, Rotate, Flip, Exposure/Contrast, Format/Compression):
   - Keep two figures: BEFORE and AFTER.
   - Write a one-sentence summary in the figcaption.
   - Fill in the meta list (dimensions, format, file size).
4) Publish:
   - Push to GitHub.
   - Upload to your MCC cPanel and share the public link in Canvas.
     Example: http://username.mccweb.net/optimization/index.html

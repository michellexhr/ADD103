Matched Trilogy

I started reading these books in middle school but I never got the chance to finish them until about 1-2 years ago. This series takes place an a Dystopian World. These young adults get matched to their life long partner at 17 years old. When Cassia goes home after getting matched to her childhood best friend, the image of another childhood friend flashes on the screen. After this unlikely glitch Cassia starts questioning if the Society is everything it seems to be.

Zodiac Academy

Honestly I think this is my favorite series I have read so far. I have finished reading the first 3 books and about to finish the fourth one. I usually like to read physical copies but I got a kindle for my birthday and have been putting good use to it. This series follows Darcy and Tory Vega as they discover they are lost fae princesses and learn how to harness their powers at Zodiac Academy, all while trying to defend the throne from the four heirs who want to claim it.

Silent Patient

This is my favorite standalone Psychological Thriller book! This book is about a Psychotherapist whose name is Theo Faber. He becomes obsessed with treating a famous artist whose name is, Alicia Berenson, who went mute after she murdered her husband. The story follows Dr. Faber's investigation to Alicia's silence, which uncovers dark secrets from both of their lives.

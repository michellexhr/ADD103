Thriller

A thriller's main goal is to create tension and excitement from beginning to end. It's known for its fast pace, suspenseful plots, and heart-racing twists. These books are intended to keep readers on the edge of their seats, always wondering what will happen next. The settings in these types of stories can vary from big cities to remote places to mainly quiet small towns. It doesn't matter what the setting is; there is always an eerie feeling that something could go wrong at any given moment. Thriller books usually follow characters in dangerous or mysterious situations, seeking to uncover secrets, solve a crime, or even escape a deadly threat.

Mystery

Mystery novels focus on solving puzzles, uncovering secrets, and revealing hidden truths. These stories often feature detectives, investigators, or ordinary characters who must piece together clues to solve a crime or uncover a conspiracy. Mystery books keep readers engaged with suspenseful twists, red herrings, and surprising revelations. They challenge the mind and keep readers guessing until the very end, offering satisfaction when the puzzle is finally solved.
